# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2025

## Introduction

Sugumemo ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes what information the App collects and how it is used.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information Stored in the App

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

- **Memo and reminder content**: Text you enter for memos and reminders
- **Creation date and time**: When memos and reminders were created
- **Scheduled notification time**: When reminders are set to trigger
- **Status information**: Reminder state (scheduled, completed, expired)
- **Settings**: Such as the last preset time used

This information is stored in local storage (AsyncStorage) on your device and is not sent to external servers.

### 1.2 Use of Permissions

The App uses the following permissions:

- **Microphone**: Required for voice input. Audio is processed on the device and not sent to external servers.
- **Speech recognition**: Required to convert speech to text. Recognition is performed using the device’s capabilities and is not sent to external servers.
- **Notifications**: Required to deliver reminder notifications.

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect:

- **Device information**: Device type, OS version, language settings, etc. (to the extent necessary for the App to function)
- **Advertising identifier**: Used for ad delivery via Google AdMob (see the "Advertising" section)

## 2. How We Use Information

### 2.1 Providing App Features

Collected information is used only for:

- Providing memo and reminder features
- Providing notifications
- Providing voice input
- Technical processing necessary for the App to function

### 2.2 Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use the following for ad delivery and measurement:

- Advertising identifier (IDFA/AAID)
- Device information
- Ad impressions and clicks

For AdMob’s privacy policy, see [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 2.3 In-App Purchases (Subscription)

The App uses RevenueCat to process purchases and restore purchases for the paid plan (Pro). Transaction data is processed through the App Store or Google Play, and RevenueCat may be used to verify purchase status. For RevenueCat’s privacy practices, see [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

## 3. Sharing of Information

The App does not share your personal information with third parties except:

- **Advertising**: For ad delivery via Google AdMob
- **Payment processing**: For purchase verification via RevenueCat
- **Legal requirements**: When required by law, regulation, or legal process

## 4. Data Storage and Deletion

### 4.1 Where Data Is Stored

All memo and reminder data is stored only on your device and is not sent to external servers.

### 4.2 Automatic Deletion

The App automatically deletes data under the following conditions:

- **Reminders**: Automatically deleted 30 days after the scheduled notification date
- **Maximum stored items**: When the count exceeds 50, older data is deleted first

### 4.3 Manual Deletion

You can delete all data by uninstalling the App.

## 5. Data Security

The App protects your data by:

- Storing data only in local storage on the device
- Not sending memo content to external servers
- Using encrypted storage where applicable

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated without notice. For material changes, we will notify you in the App or update this page.
